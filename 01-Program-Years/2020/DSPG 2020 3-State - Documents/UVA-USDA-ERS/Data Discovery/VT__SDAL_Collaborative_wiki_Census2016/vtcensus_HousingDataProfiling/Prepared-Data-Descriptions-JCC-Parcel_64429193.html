This page provides basic descriptions of the key variables used in this study after they were prepared.

Datasets for Residential Parcels

Location: Information on the physical location of the parcel

Characteristics: Information on the physical characteristics of the improvement on residential parcels

Selling: Information on when a property changed ownership
