Notes:

  • Bolded: ACS 90% MOE crosses 0 (no data still with MOE)
  • Arlington County data is weighted by the number of units within the property. 
    • Not all properties have a units counts, which drives the number of housing units down (especially when the NA is for a multifamily)
    • Number of unknown units count: 206 (2009), 233 (2010), 237 (2011), 248 (2012) and 463 (2013)
  • The total estimate below do not include those with an NA value (which are weighted). The % NA are based on the total estimate with NA values
  • Arlington County defines this category as Heating Type vs the ACS definition of Heating Fuel
    • Unknown category for Arlington County are properties that could not be placed into one of the ACS bins due to lack of specificity
  • All prepared Arlington data are u sed n the tabulations
