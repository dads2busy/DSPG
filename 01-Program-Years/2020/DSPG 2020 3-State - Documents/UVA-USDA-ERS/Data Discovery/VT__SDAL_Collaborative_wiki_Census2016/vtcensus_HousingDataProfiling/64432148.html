VT Census Case Studies : ACS Benchmarking: B25102 Real Estate Taxes Paid (ACREA)

Created and last modified by molfe on 04 Jan 2016

Notes:

  • Bolded: ACS 90% MOE crosses 0 (no data still with MOE)
  • Arlington County does not include Apartment/Multifamily residents; only single family or condos have bedroom information
  • Original ACS table was broken down by those with a mortgage and those without a mortgage. As the Arlington County data does not have mortgage information, this table was aggregated up
  • No real estate taxes paid for Arlington County include those parcels that had taxes levied but the amount was deferred/given as relief and those parcel that had taxes levied but had no record of paying the taxes
  • 2009 is not include as the same table is not available for that year (Arlington County's 2009 estimates are included in the creation of the 5 year.)
    • 2009 payment data for 2009 is 50% smaller than the other 4 years, which lowers the estimates for the 5 Year Estimates
  • 5 year estimates are in constant 2013 dollars
  • All prepared Arlington County data are used in the tabulations. When calculating census tract and block group (bg) comparisons, only those data that are within a designated tract/bg are included. When geocoding addresses along the county boarder, rooftop coordinates can occur outside the county. When merging tract to coordinates, these properties would not receive a tract/BG ID number
