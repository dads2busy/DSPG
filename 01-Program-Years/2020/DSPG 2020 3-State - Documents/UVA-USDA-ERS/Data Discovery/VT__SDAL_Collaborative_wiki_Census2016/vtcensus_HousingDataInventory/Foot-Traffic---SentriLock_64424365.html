VT Census Case Studies : Foot Traffic - SentriLock

Created and last modified by molfe on 02 Jan 2016

Brief Overall Description of the Dataset:

“Every month SentriLock, LLC. provides NAR Research with data on the number of properties shown by a REALTOR®. Lockboxes made by SentriLock, LLC. are used in roughly a third of home showings across the nation.” NAR states this measure is a strong leading indicator of contract activity one to two months down the road. Emailed Ken Fears of SentriLock about data access and he provided much of information below.

Link: http://economistsoutlook.blogs.realtor.org/tag/foot-traffic/
