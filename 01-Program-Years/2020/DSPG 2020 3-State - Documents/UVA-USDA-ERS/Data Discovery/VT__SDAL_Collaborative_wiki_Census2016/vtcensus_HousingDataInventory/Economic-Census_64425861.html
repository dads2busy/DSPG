VT Census Case Studies : Economic Census

Created and last modified by molfe on 02 Jan 2016

Brief Overall Description of the Dataset:

The Economic Census is comprised of survey results from nearly 4 million businesses across the United States. The dataset reports the economic conditions of the United States of America. Data is most recently available for the year of 2012. From this data, the government and researchers can measure the health of the US Economy and work to provide aid where needed.

Link: http://www.census.gov/econ/census/about/
