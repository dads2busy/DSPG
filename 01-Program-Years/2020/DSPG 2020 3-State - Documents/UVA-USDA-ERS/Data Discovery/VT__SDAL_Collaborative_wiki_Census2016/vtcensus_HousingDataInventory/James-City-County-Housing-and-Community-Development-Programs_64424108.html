Brief Overall Description of the Dataset:

This dataset is a compilation of public record housing permits. The data is collected as permits are requested and given. Therefore, there is not a full dataset for any specific year, just a dataset up to a particular year. This dataset gives the history of every building permitted in James City County.

Link: http://www.jamescitycountyva.gov/HousingCommunityDev/index.html
