VT Census Case Studies : Residential Finance Survey (RFS)

Created by molfe on 11 Aug 2015

Brief Overall Description of the Dataset:

The Census Bureau has taken the Residential Finance Survey (RFS) as part of the decennial census since 1950. The RFS is a unique survey for several reasons:

  • It collects, tabulates, and presents data for properties, the standard unit of reference for financial transactions related to housing.

  • It is the only source of information on property, mortgage, and financial characteristics for multi-unit rental properties.

  • It conducts interviews of property owners and mortgage lenders, resulting in more accurate information on property and mortgage characteristics.

  • As part of the decennial census, it is mandatory.

  • It is able to subdivide the industry into relevant components.

For methodology, see: http://www.census.gov/housing/rfs/methodology/
