VT Census Case Studies : RS Metrics

Created and last modified by molfe on 09 Aug 2015

Brief Overall Description of the Dataset:

RS Metrics provides investor and corporate clients with proprietary insights and data based on quantitative analysis of satellite and aerial imagery, overlaid with other open source information such as store locations, demographics and climate data. The company also provides data for other industry sectors, such as distribution, transportation, shipping, mining, and energy, as well as macroeconomic indicators, such as GDP and employment. RS Metrics partnerships with leading satellite imagery companies provide us with global coverage, cutting edge technical capabilities, and access to 5+ years of imagery archives for historical analysis. RS Metrics provides data that can be used alone or with other data sources to measure and track activity, change, and progress in regional or local economies and markets.

Company was contact and an information/POW document was sent

For each Census Tract,the following progress and activity at individual development cab be provided in a shapefile-based dataset with the following attributes: Type of development (housing or apartment),  Number and type of housing (single family, townhouse), Stage of construction (grading, foundations, building construction, completed), Estimated number of bedrooms or apartments, Presence of cars on roads, driveways, parking lots, Image date and time, lat/long coordinates, zip codes, Census Tract number.

This document was for data from 2010-2015 (for lower price can ask for 2010-2014).
