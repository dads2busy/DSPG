Brief Overall Description of the Dataset:

Axiometrics the leading provider of apartment data and market research only multifamily research provider to survey every property in its database at the floor plan level every month. We track more details, more often, on tens of thousands of properties in hundreds of U.S. markets and their submarkets, equating to millions of units and beds for conventional apartment and student housing properties. They conduct frequent surveys of properties and markets and blend with demographic and economic indicators to give the granular information to make investment decisions. They call every apartment in their database, every month, and obtain rents, occupancy and concessions at the floor-plan level. Data include conventional apartments, student housing, and real estate investment trusts.
