Brief Overall Description of the Dataset:

The mapping center compiles and manages Arlington County's geo-coded data. In addition general shapefiles, they do create datasets and shapefiles for project specific tasks when asked.

Purpose

  • What is the purpose of the organization collecting the data?

The GIS Mapping Center serves as the County's official mapping and analysis unit providing GIS consulting services,  cartographic expertise, aerial photo analysis, data maintenance, and data conversion support for all County agencies.  The GIS Mapping Center works with agency data owners to maintain corporate data including real estate, zoning, census, planimetric, street map, and street network to name but a few.  

  • Why is it collected and how does the organization use it?

Data is primarily disseminated to County government offices 

  • Who else uses the data?

The data is used throughout the county in addition to researchers, citizens, and businesses
