VT Census Case Studies : Arlington County Affordable Housing Study: Resident Poll Results

Created by aerogers, last modified by molfe on 02 Jan 2016

Brief Overall Description of the Dataset:

In order to better understand the feelings of their constituents regarding affordable housing, Arlington, Co. public officials conducted a phone survey in 2014.  Questions focus on the responders thoughts and opinions regarding the affordability of their current housing and what ideas they have for future housing they may have in Arlington, Co. This dataset was compiled in order to show trends in opinions across the county. There is separate data for homeowners, renters and people currently working in Arlington, Co. but not currently living in Arlington. Co.
