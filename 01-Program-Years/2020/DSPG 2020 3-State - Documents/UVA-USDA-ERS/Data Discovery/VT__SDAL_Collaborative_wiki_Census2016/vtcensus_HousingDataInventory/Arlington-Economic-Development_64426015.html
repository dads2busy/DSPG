Purpose

  • What is the purpose of the organization collecting the data?

Arlington Economic Development (AED) is dedicated to the preservation and enhancement of an economically competitive and sustainable community by providing aggressive leadership and superior services to Arlington’s arts and cultural community, the business community, tourism industry and real estate development.

Services provided by AED:

  1.  Community development updates
  2. Competitive industry trends and intelligence
  3. Economic/demographic trends and business tax and analysis
  4. County-sponsored research on emerging business and industry trends
  5. Public brand building and exposure
  6. Awards nomination and recognition opportunities
  7. Meeting and conference location specialists
  8. Site location and expansion facilitation
  9. Benefits and incentives for eligible businesses
  10. Planning, permitting, and zoning liaisons for complex projects

  • Why is it collected and how does the organization use it?

The data is collected for tax, free, and/or planning purposes, depending on the specific data.

  • Who else uses the data?

In raw form, the data is used throughout the county. The data also forms the basis of other commercial and federal databases.

  • Who do they sell the data to?

Commercial/non profit entities who repurpose the data to create their own databases or products.
